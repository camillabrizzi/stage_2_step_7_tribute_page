My Chemical Fandom

Reasons why My Chemical Romance are still my favourite band

Five reasons why I cannot get bored of them:

1. They still rock!

My Chemical Romance got reunited almost 3 years ago and have been touring again after postponing their shows due to the pandemic. They released a new single, 'Decay', in May 2022 and it's a banger! You can listen to it on this page.

2. They first got me into coding - kinda.

MySpace and VampireFreaks (ha!) gave me a first insight into HTML when I was a teen. I got to practice a little coding whilst I was an admin of the Italian My Chemical Romance fan-club. It was a big deal!

3. Nostalgia...

They say 'once an emo, always an emo'. I may have changed my wardrobe and makeup habits since (black is still my favourite colour), but my heart still flutters every time I hear their songs. Teenage angst, x-rated fanfictions, so many memories! MCR got me out of dark moments of my life and allowed me to chat to people all over the world.

4. They're simply iconic.

Their lyrics, music videos, fashion... Need I say more

5. I finally saw them live!

May 28th 2022 I realised my life-long dream of seeing them live, in Cardiff. The gig was all that I expected and more! Had an absolutely banging time, surrounded by emo oldies and got to overdo my eyeliner once again. I sang my heart out and cried too, of course. I cannot wait for their next tour. I remember when they came to Italy and my mother thought I was too young to travel to Milan to see them on my own. I cried and raged for a long time (sorry ma!).
